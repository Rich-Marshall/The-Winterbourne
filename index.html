Emie Hawkshaw


Emie has ample, appropriate experience in the hospitality and licensed trade and a wealth of transferrable skills and is confident in her ability to establish and sustain business in all her previous roles.


After working in customer-service related positions for the last 18 years ranging from managing a bespoke Sunglasses outlet to Michelin and 3 rosette Restaurants She found that her real passion lay in Hospitality.


Emie has spent nearly a decade in the catering industry, in this time she has developed an understanding that every establishment is different and therefore different approaches are often required. Clearly, she has a thorough working knowledge of all elements of running a pub/bar. She has proved herself to be capable of retaining a creative and competitive edge in the marketplace ensuring she meets the needs and expectations of the customers.

Roger Hawkshaw


Chef patron at The Winterbourne, Winterbourne Bassett. Roger prides himself in using fresh seasonal produce, quality meats and fish. His perpetually evolving menu is testament to this.


Fortunate to work in some high-end restaurants and hotels. Roger's career really took off when he was appointed as junior sous at the 3aa rosette Kitley Hotel under James Tanner (Tanner Brothers). A Roux Scholarship followed with a two-year placement at the Relais Chateau, Lake Plaid Lodge in North State New York USA. Roger also worked at the 1 Michelin Star, New Angel as Senior Sous to John Burton-Race. For More than two Years.


Moving to Wiltshire he gained a great reputation at the Barbury Inn, receiving 10/10 review from Wiltshire Life’s Magazines Secret Diner, ranking 2nd in the Wiltshire Life Magazines Pub Of the Year. Roger then took the position of head chef at The White Horse Compton Bassett Gaining 2aa Rosettes & Michelin Recognition. He now has his eyes firmly on the future of The Winterbourne.

The Coffee Shop


During the first lockdown of 2020, we were discussing ways we could diversify our business and adapt the space available to us at The Winterbourne. After some deliberation and to meet a local need, we decided to turn our conservatory into a coffee shop!


It’s been a real success with locals and we offer a selection of delicious, freshly baked cakes and savouries, coffees and teas and light refreshments throughout the day, from 11am, seven days a week.

Blanche


After the pandemic hit in early 2020 and all the uncertainty and upheaval that it brought to our world, we looked at expanding our operation to diversify and keep the business ticking over. After much searching, we found our ideal candidate, the beautiful ‘Blanche’ a 1972/73 Citroen H van.


She is kitted out with a wood fired oven, making her the perfect vehicle for selling takeaways in addition to dine in. We are currently experimenting with what can be created in the oven from savoury & sweet pizzas to BBQ pork ribs. Keep an eye out for updates!
